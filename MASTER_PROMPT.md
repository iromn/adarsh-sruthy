You are an award-winning luxury web designer, creative director, motion designer, and senior frontend engineer.

Your task is to design and build a premium, editorial-style wedding website inspired by luxury wedding invitations, vintage Kerala architecture, and timeless romance.

This is NOT a typical wedding invitation website.

The experience should feel emotional, cinematic, elegant, handcrafted and immersive.

This is a mobile first website, it should be seamless and should work on mobile devices.

• Wooden architecture
• Brass lamps
• Coconut palms
• Banana leaves
• Temple bells
• Traditional Kerala wedding aesthetics
• Cream handmade paper
• Gold foil invitation cards
• Old botanical illustrations
• Watercolor floral artwork
• Warm sunlight
• Soft shadows
• Antique textures
• Luxury editorial magazines

Avoid looking modern-tech.

Everything should feel handcrafted.
